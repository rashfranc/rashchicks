Sample Free Poultry Training Offered

Disease Prevention

Vaccination, Deworming, and Disease Curing

We educate farmers on vaccination against Fowl Pox, Newcastle, Gumboro, Marek's, and Samonella diseases among many others. We also encourage proper deworming at the right age. We also educate our farmers on isolation of sick birds and how to treat their sick birds.

Feeding Guide

Optimal Feeding Schedule for Improved Kienyeji Chicks

We guide farmers on appropriate amounts of feeds to avoid both over and under feeding their chicks. Advice on the type of feeds, feed changing formula, and feeders plus drinkers is offered too.

Poultry Best Practices

What to Do and When to Do It

We advice both new and experienced farmers on the best practices they should put either in or around the coop, on feeding and watering, on shading, on spacing, on medication, and general coop safety for the least case of incurring losses.

Frequently Asked Questions

About Rash Chicks & Poultry

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Rash Chicks & Poultry began with a simple mission: to provide small-scale farmers with high-quality, pre-vaccinated chickens that would guarantee short-term profits with minimal risk and cost.

What started as a small operation in Mwingi has now grown to serve 9 counties across Kenya, helping hundreds of farmers establish profitable poultry supply businesses.

Our Approach

We don't just sell chicks - we provide complete farming and business solutions:

  • Pre-vaccinated, disease-resistant, and healthy chicks,
  • Comprehensive free training programs to our farmers,
  • Ongoing veterinary support from certified vets, and
  • Detailed farm best practices for minimum losses.
